C477 718.403(2) Phase Declaration 

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to properly describe in the declaration of condominium the membership vote and ownership in the association attributable to each unit in each phase, in violation of section 718.403(2)(e), Florida Statutes.

NOTE:  Amendment to declaration of condominium can be inserted for declaration of condominium.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.403(2)(e), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Is this a phase condominium?

Does the declaration of condominium describe the membership vote and ownership in the association attributable to each unit in each phase?
