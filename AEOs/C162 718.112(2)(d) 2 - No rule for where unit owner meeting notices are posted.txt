C162 718.112(2)(d)2 Notice requirements



Allegation:  The board failed to adopt a rule regarding where unit owner meeting notices should be posted, in violation of section 718.112(2)(d) 2, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(d) 2, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Where are unit owner meeting notices posted?
Does the association have a written rule regarding where the notices are posted?
