C147 718.112(20(c) Failure to ratify emergency action
 


Allegation:  The board failed to ratify emergency action at next meeting, in violation of section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Did the board take emergency action at a meeting?

Did the agenda include ratification of the emergency action?

Did the meeting minutes reflect that the board ratified the emergency action?
