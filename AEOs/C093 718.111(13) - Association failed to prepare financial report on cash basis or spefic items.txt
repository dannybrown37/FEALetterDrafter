C093 718.111(13) FS. Reporting

Allegation 1: The association failed to prepare the annual financial report on a cash basis and/or failed to include in the annual financial report specified receipt or expenditure line items, or disclosures on limited common elements in violation of Section 718.111(13) Florida Statute.

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.111(13) FS.

Elements:
* Does the association make less than $100,000 in revenues?
* Does the association operate less than 50 units? 
* Was a report of cash receipts and disbursements generated?
* If created was any category left off?
* Were reserves included?
* Did the association elect to create financial statements instead?

Evidence:
Declaration of Condominium
Financial Report
Budget
Bank Statements
Billing Statements
