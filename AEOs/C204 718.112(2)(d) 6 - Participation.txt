C204 718.112(2) (d) 6 Participation

Allegation:  The board failed to allow unit owners to speak on agenda items during a meeting of the unit owners, in violation of section 718.112(2) (d) 6, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.112(2) (d) 6, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Did a unit owners' meeting take place?

When did the unit owners' meeting occur?

Was there an agenda for the meeting?

Were one or more unit owners denied the right to speak on the agenda items?

Do you have the names and contact information for any witnesses that can verify that a unit owner was not allowed to speak?

Evidence Needed:

A copy of the meeting notice, including the agenda

A copy of the meeting minutes

Witness statements from unit owners
