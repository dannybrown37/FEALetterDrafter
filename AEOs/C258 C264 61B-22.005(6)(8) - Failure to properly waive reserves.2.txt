C258 C264 718.112(2)(f)2, Failure to follow proper method to waive or reduce reserve funding. 

Allegation: Specifically, the complainant alleges that the 2005 assessment is based upon the reduced reserve funding of the prior year.

Statute/Rule Reference:  
Section 718.112(2)(f)2, Florida Statutes
Rule 61B-22.005(6)(8), Florida Administrative Code

Elements:  
* Was a budget proposed/adopted?
* Was the required reserve schedule included?
* Was there an annual vote of a majority of all voting interests to waive or reduce reserves?

Evidence Needed:
Adopted Budget
Meeting minutes
