C117 61B-22.0062(2)(b) Failure to include a statement of total cash payments made by the Developer to the Association in the turnover financial statements.

Allegation: The financial statements at turnover did not include the amount of total cash payments made by the developer to the association.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Rule 61B-22.0062(2)(b), FAC.

Elements:  
* Did the Audit reflect such assessments/payments made by the Developer?
* Does this audit reflect the entire period covered?
* Is the amount in question correct?
* Does the developer claim to have paid common expenses that are not reflected on the books and records of the association?  If should is the amount and purpose disclosed for each separately?

Evidence Needed:
Turnover Audit
Yearly Audits since inception
Book and records
