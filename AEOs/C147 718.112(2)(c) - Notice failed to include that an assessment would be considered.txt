C147 718.112(20(c) Notice failed to indicate that assessment would be considered




Allegation:  Failure to include on meeting notice that an assessment would be considered, in violation of section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes

Elements:


Was a notice posted in the designated location on the condominium property?

When was the notice posted?

When was the board/committee meeting held?

Did the notice include an agenda?

Did the agenda note that an assessment would be considered?

Did the meeting minutes reflect that an assessment was considered?
