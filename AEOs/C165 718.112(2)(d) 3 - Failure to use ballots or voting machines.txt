C165 718.112(2)(d)3 Failure to use ballots or voting machine




Allegation:  Failure to use ballots or voting machines for the annual election, in violation of section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was the second notice of election sent to the owners?

Did the notice include a ballot and inner & outer envelopes?

Were ballots available at the annual meeting?

If applicable, were voting machines available at the annual meeting?
