C090 C093 718.111(13)  Failure to timely provide the annual financial report 

Allegation: The association failed to provide the end of the year financial statements or a notice to unit owners that they were available, without charge, upon written request, within 120 days after the end of the fiscal year end.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(13), Florida Statutes

Elements:  
* Determine the Association's fiscal year end or the date provided in the bylaws? 
* Did the Association prepare and complete or contract for the preparation and completion of a financial report within 90 days after fiscal year end?
* Did the Association provide a copy of the financial report or a notice that a copy will be provided upon written request not later 120 days after fiscal year end?

Evidence Needed:
Financial Report
