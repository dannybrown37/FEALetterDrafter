C045 718.111(1)(a)  Failure to maintain corporate status

Allegation :  The Respondent failed to maintain the Association's corporate status, in violation of Section 718.111(1)(a), Florida Statutes.  

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(1)(a), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

On what date was the condominium created as evidenced by the filing of the Declaration
of Condominium with the Clerk of Court?

Does the Secretary of State show the Association's corporate status as active since the
condominium's inception?
