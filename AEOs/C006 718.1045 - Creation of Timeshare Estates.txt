C006 718.1045 Creation of timeshare Estates

Allegation:  Respondent created timeshare estates in a condominium contrary to the provisions of the Declaration of Condominium, in violation of section 718.1045, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.1045, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Have timeshare estates been created?

Does the declaration allow for the creation of timeshare estates?

Were the timeshare estates created in accordance with the Declaration of Condominium?
