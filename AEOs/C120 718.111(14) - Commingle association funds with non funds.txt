C120 718.111(14), FS. Commingle

Allegation 1: The association Commingled association funds with non-association funds in violation of Section 718.111(14), Florida Statute. 

Statute/Rule Reference: Section 718.111(14), FS. 

Elements:
* Are association funds maintained separately from non association funds? 
* How are funds distinguished on Budget?
* How are funds distinguished on financial statement?
* Are funds being invested with other funds for investment purposes?
* What do bank statements indicate?

Evidence:
Bank records
Budget
Financial Statements
Declaration of Condominium
