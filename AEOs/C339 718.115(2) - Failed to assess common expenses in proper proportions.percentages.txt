C339 718.115(2) Assessment Share 

Allegation:  The association failed to assess payment of common expenses in proper proportions or percentages, in violation of section 718.115(2), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.115(2), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Were unit owners assessed for common expenses?

Was the assessment amount determined by proportions or percentages?

Were unit owners properly assessed according to these proportions or percentages?
