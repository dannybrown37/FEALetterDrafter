C516 718.502(1)(b) Closings prior to acceptance

Allegation:  The developer closed on units prior to the division accepting the condominium filing.

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.502(1)(b), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Has the condominium been filed with the Division?

When was the filing received?

When was the filing accepted?

Did the developer close on the sale of units prior to the Division accepting filing?


Evidence Needed:

Documentation showing condominium was filed with the Division

Documentation showing the filing was accepted by the Division

Copy of deeds
