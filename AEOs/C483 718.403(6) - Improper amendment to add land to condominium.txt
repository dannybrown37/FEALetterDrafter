C483 718.403(6) Additional Lands

Allegation:  The Respondent amended the declaration of condominium to add land to the condominium, in violation of section 718.403(6), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.403(6), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Was there an amendment adding additional land to the condominium?

Does the original declaration of condominium grant the right to add additional land to the condominium?

Is the amendment adding additional land to the condominium consistent with the provisions of the declaration granting such right?
