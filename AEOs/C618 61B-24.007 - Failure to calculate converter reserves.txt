C618 61B-24.007 Failure to calculate converter reserves properly

Allegation:  Developer improperly calculated the converter reserves

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.618(1), FS, rule 61B-24.007, FAC

Elements:
* Which reserves accounts are required?  
* Were any warranties offered, if reserve account is not funded?
* Was a surety bond posted?
* What type of roof?  Was right numerator and denominator used?
* Was the right numerator and denominator used for the air conditioner? (1000 yards from seacoast?)
* Utilized the converter reserve worksheet (Excel document).

Evidence Needed:
Declaration
Conversion Report
Budget
Accounting records showing converter reserves fund balances
