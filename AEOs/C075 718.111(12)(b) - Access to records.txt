C075 718.111(12)(b) Failure to provide access to records

Allegation:    The Respondent failed to provide access to the  official association records within five (5) business days of a written request from a unit owner or authorized representative, in violation of section 718.111(12)(b), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(12)(b), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Did the unit owner or authorized representative make a written request to inspect the Association records?  Yes. 

Was the unit owner or authorized representative denied the right to inspect the records? No. Complainant failed to provide a date and time to conduct his records inspection.

Was the right to inspect the records granted within five business days of the Association's receipt of the written request?  No. Complainant failed to provide a date and time to conduct his records inspection.
