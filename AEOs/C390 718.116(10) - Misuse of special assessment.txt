C390 718.116(10), FS. Special Assessments

Allegation 1: The association failed to use special assessment funds for intended purposes in violation of 718.116(10), Florida Statute.

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.116(10), FS.

Elements:
* What was the special assessment assessed for?
* Was the money spent on the purpose?
* Was the remainder credited to the account of the owner or refunded back?
* Did the notice to collect the funds state the purpose of the special assessment? 

Evidence:
Financial Statement
Bank Records 
Billing Statement
