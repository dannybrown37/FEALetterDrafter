C072 718.112(12)(a) Failure to maintain records

Allegation:  The association failed to maintain its official records, in violation of section 718.111(12)(a), Florida Statutes.  Specifically, the association failed to maintain or annually update the question and answer sheet*. 

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(12)(a), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Was the record requested an official record of the association? Yes

Did complainant attend a records inspection to know that the records in question are not being maintained?

Were the records maintained?

Evidence Needed:
The alleged records that are not being maintained
