C330 718.115(1)(d) Cable TV

Allegation:  The association failed to discontinue the master antenna television system or duly franchised cable television service for a hearing-impaired or legally blind unit owner, in violation of section 718.115(1)(d)2., Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.115(1)(d)2., Florida Statutes

Elements:

Has the association entered into a master television service contract? 

Does a hearing-impaired or legally blind unit owner receive the service?

Did the hearing-impaired or legally blind unit owner request that the service be discontinued?

Is/was the hearing-impaired or legally blind unit owner still being charged for the service after requesting it be discontinued?
