C090 C093 718.111(13) Failure to timely provide the annual financial report.

Allegation:  The association did not provide the annual financial report in the time frame allowed.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(13), FS

Elements:
* When was the end of the fiscal year?
* Do the bylaws provide for a date requirement for the annual report?
* When should have the report been available, pursuant to the fiscal year and statute?
* What date was report made available?
* Was a notice, instead of report, sent to unit owners regarding the availability of the report?
* Was the information mailed or hand delivered?

Evidence Needed:
Bylaws
Financial report
Postmark from complainant's report
