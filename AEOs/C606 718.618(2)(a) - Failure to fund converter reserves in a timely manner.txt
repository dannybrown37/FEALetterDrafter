C606 718.618(2)(a) Failure to fund converter reserves in a timely manner

Allegation:  Developer has been improperly funding converter reserves

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.618(2)(a), FS

Elements:
* Which reserves accounts are required?  
* Were any warranties offered, if reserve account is not funded?
* Was a surety bond posted?
* What type of roof?  Was right numerator and denominator used?
* Utilized the converter reserve worksheet (Excel document).
* What reserves funded on a pro rata basis upon the sale of each unit.
* How many sales have taken place.

Evidence Needed:
Conversion Report
Budget
Accounting records showing converter reserves fund balances
Year end Audit
Information on sales that have taken place.
