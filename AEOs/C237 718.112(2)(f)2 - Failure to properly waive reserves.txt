C237 718.112(2)(f)2.FS, 61B-22.005(6), (8), FAC. Reserves

Allegation 1: The association failed to follow proper method to waive or reduce reserve funding in violation of section 718.112(2) (f) 2.Florida Statutes, and 61B-22.005(6), (8), Florida Administrative Code.

Statute/Rule Reference: 718.112(2)(f)2.FS / 61B-22.005(6), (8), FAC.

Elements:
* Was there a vote by a majority of the unit owners to waive reserve funding? 
* Was the vote for the current year?
* In multi condominiums did the vote include representation from each condominium so that a majority vote or quorum could be established?
* Did the Developer vote?
* Was the developer allowed to vote?
* If the developer is no longer in control were the non developer controlled interest represent sufficiently?

Evidence:
Minutes
Financial Statements
Budget
Declaration of Condominium
