C090 718.111(13) Failure to timely provide the annual financial report

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to mail or hand deliver to each unit owner, no later than 120 days after the end of the fiscal year or other date as provided in the bylaws, the year-end financial statements for the preceding fiscal year, in violation of section 718.111(13), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(13), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

What is the end of association's fiscal year?  

Was the financial report mailed within 120 days of the end of the fiscal year?
OR
Was a notification that the financial report was available upon request mailed within 120 days of the end of the fiscal year?
