C126 Section 718.112(20(a)2  Failure to Provide a timely or Substantive response



Allegation:  The board failed to provide a substantive response to a written inquiry, in violation of section 718.112(2)(a) 2, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(a) 2, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Did the unit owner send the board a letter of inquiry?
Was the letter sent via certified mail?
Did the board receive the letter?
If yes, when did the board receive the letter?
Did the board respond in writing within 30 days of receipt of the inquiry?
Did the response from the board answer the question(s) in the inquiry?
