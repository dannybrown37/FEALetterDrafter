C087 61B-22.002 Failure to maintain sufficient accounting records

Allegation: Failure to maintain sufficient accounting records. 
 

Statute/Rule Reference:  Rule 61B-22.002, Florida Administrative Code

Elements:  
* Were accounting records maintained in sufficient detail to permit determination of the revenues and expenses or receipts and disbursements attributable to separate condominiums or operating and reserve funds?

Evidence Needed:
Accounting records
