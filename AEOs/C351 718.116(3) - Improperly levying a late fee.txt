C351 718.116(3) Improperly levying a late fee

Allegation:  The Respondent levied an administrative late fee without having the authority to do so listed in the Declaration of Condominium or by-laws, in violation of section 718.116(3), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.116(3), Florida Statutes

Elements:

When are assessments due, i.e. quarterly or monthly?

Was the complaining unit owner late in paying the required assessment or assessments?


Was a late fee added to the past due assessment amount?

Do the association's documents give the board the authority to levy a late fee?
