C258 718.112(2)(f)2 FS., 61B-22.005(6), FAC.  Reserves

Allegation 1: The association failed to calculate to fund reserves in a timely manner or fully fund reserves in violation of Section 718.112(2)(f)2 Florida Statutes, and 61B-22.005(6), FAC.

Statute/Rule Reference: 718.112(2)(f)2 FS., 61B-22.005(6), FAC.  

Elements:
* Have reserves been waived or reduced?
* Are reserves funded with the same frequency that assessments are collected?
* Does operating fund owe reserve fund?

Evidence:
Financial Statements
Budget
Bank Records
Declaration of Condominium
Minutes
