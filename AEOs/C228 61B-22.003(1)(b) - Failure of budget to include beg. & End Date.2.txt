C228 61B-22.003(1)(b)  Failure to disclose the beginning and ending dates of the period covered by the proposed budget.

Allegation: Specifically, the complainant alleges that the proposed budget fails to show the dates covered by the budget. 

Statute/Rule Reference:  Rule 61B-22.003(1)(b), FAC

Elements:  
* Was a budget proposed?
* Did the budget contain the beginning and ending dates of the period covered by the proposed budget?
* What is the Association's fiscal year.

Evidence Needed:
Proposed Budget
Condominium documents pertaining to fiscal year.
