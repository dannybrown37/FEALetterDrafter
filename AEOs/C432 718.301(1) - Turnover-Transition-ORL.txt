C432-718.301(1)-Turnover-Transition

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to turn over control of the board of administration of the association, in violation of section 718.301(1), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.301(1), Florida Statutes.

Elements:
1. Do unit owners other than the developer own 15 percent or more of the units? 
2. How many board members are there? 
3. Have the unit owners other than the developer elected not less than one-third of the members of the board of administration of the association? If not, why?
4. Unit owners other than the developer are entitled to elect not less than a majority of the members of the board of administration of an association when the conditions stated in 718.301(1)(a-e) have been met. Have the conditions been met? If not, which condition has not been met?
