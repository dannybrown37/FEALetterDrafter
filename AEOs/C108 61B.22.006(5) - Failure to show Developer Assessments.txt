C108 61B-22.006(5) Failure to show developer assessments

Allegation: The financial report did not disclose the assessment revenues from the developer separately from that of the non-developer owners

Statute/Rule Reference:  Rule 61B-22.006(5), FAC.

Elements:  
* Was there a guarantee in place?
* Were accounting records maintained in sufficient detail to permit determination of the revenues attributable to the Developer as well as the non-developer unit owners? 
* Did the Audit reflect such assessments made by the Developer?

Evidence Needed:
Documents indicating the Developer commitment.
Accounting books and records.
Year end financial report
