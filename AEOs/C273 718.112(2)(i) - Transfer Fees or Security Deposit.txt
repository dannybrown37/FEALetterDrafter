C273 718.112(2)(i) Transfer fee without authority



Allegation:  The board levied a transfer fee or security deposit without having authorization, in violation of section 718.112(2)(i), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(i), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was a transfer fee or security deposit levied?
Do the condominium documents allow for a transfer fee or security deposit?
Was the transfer fee or security deposit paid?
