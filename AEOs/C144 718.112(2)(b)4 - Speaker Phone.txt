C144 718.112(2) (b) 5 Speaker phone

Allegation:  The board failed to provide a speaker phone for board or committee meetings held by teleconference, in violation of section 718.112(2) (b) 5, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.112(2) (b) 5, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was a board or committee meeting held?

When was the board or committee meeting held?

Was the meeting conducted by teleconference?

Was a speaker phone provided by the board?

Is there anyone who can verify that the speaker phone was provided or not provided?

Evidence Needed:

Copy of meeting notice

Copy of meeting minutes

Witness statements confirming that a speaker phone was either provided or not provided
