C078 718.112(12)(c) Failure to provide copies of records

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to permit an association member or an authorized representative to make or obtain copies of the official association records within five working days of receipt of a written request, in violation of section 718.111(12)(c), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(12)(c), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Did the unit owner or authorized representative attempt to exercise their right to make or obtain copies of Association records? 

Was the unit owner or authorized representative denied the right to make or obtain copies of Association records? 

Was the unit owner or authorized representative permitted to make or obtain copies of the association records within five working days of the Association's receipt of their written request?
