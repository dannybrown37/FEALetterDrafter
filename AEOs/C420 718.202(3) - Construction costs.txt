C420 718.202(3) Construction costs

Allegation:  The board improperly withdrew escrow funds, in violation of section 718.202(3), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.202(3), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Did the sales contract state that the developer may withdraw escrow funds?

What was the purchase price of the unit?

How much was deposited into the escrow account?

Has the construction of improvement begun?

What were the escrow funds used for?

Evidence Needed:

A copy of the sales contract

Documentation showing that construction of improvements had begun (i.e. building permits, photos)

Documentation showing that escrow funds were withdrawn

Documentation showing what the escrow funds were used for
