C357 718.116(8) Failure to provide within 15 days a certificate showing all monies owed is paid

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to provide within fifteen (15) days of a written request a certificate, signed by an officer or agent of the Association stating all assessments or money owed by the unit owner, in violation of section 718.116(8), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.116(8), Florida Statutes

Elements:

On what date the association sent a written request for the certificate?

Did the association provide the signed certificate within 15 days of the request?
