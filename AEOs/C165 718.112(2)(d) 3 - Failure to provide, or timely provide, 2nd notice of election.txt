C165 718.112(2)(d)3 Failure to provide second notice of election



Allegation:  Failure to provide, or timely provide, second notice of election or omitting materials such as ballots, envelopes, and candidate information sheets, in violation of section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes and rule 61B-23.0021(7)(8), Florida Administrative Code.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was the second notice of election sent to the owners?

When was the second notice sent?

On what date was the election scheduled?

Did the notice include ballots, envelopes and candidate information sheets?

If no, how many candidates submitted notices?

Was this the same number or less than the number of vacancies?
