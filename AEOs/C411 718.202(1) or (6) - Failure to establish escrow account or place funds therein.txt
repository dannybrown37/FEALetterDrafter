C411 718.202(1) or (6) Failure to establish an escrow account or place fund therein

Allegation:  Developer has not properly escrowed contract and/or reservation deposits.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.201(1) or (6), FS

Elements:
* Was deposit received pursuant to sales contract or reservation agreement?
* Was project substantially complete?
* Was an alternative assurance approved by the Division?
* Was any type of receipt given to the purchaser?

Evidence Needed:
Contract or reservation agreement
Receipts of Deposit
