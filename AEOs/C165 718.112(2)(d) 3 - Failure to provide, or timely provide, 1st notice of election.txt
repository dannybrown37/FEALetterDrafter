C165 718.112(20(d)3 Failure to provide first notice of election



Allegation:  Failure to provide, or timely provide, first notice of election, in violation of section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes and rule 61B-23.0021(4), Florida Administrative Code.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was the first notice election sent to the owners?

When was the first notice sent?

On what date was the election scheduled?
