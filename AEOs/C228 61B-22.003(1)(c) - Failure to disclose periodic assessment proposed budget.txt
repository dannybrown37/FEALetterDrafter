C228 61B-22.003(1)(c) Failure to disclose periodic assessment for each unit in proposed budget.

Allegation:  Association failed to disclose the periodic assessment for each unit type in the proposed budget

Statute/Rule Reference:  Rule 61B-22.003(1)(c), FAC

Elements:
* Is the annual and periodic amount per unit type included?
* Is it stated for the period on which the assessment will be due?
* Is it based on the proportion of ownership pursuant to condominium documents?

Evidence Needed:
Proposed Budget
Declaration, Bylaws or Articles
