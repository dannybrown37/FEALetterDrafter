C093 718.111(13)(b) Failure to produce year end financial statement (cash based report)

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to produce a complete set of year end financial statements in the form of a report of cash receipts and expenditures, in violation of section 718.111(13)(b)1., Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(13)(b)1., Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Are there less than 50 units in the condominium?

Are the condominium's annual revenues less than $100,000?

What kind of year-end financial statements were produced, if any?

Did the Respondent prepare a compiled, reviewed or audited financial statement in lieu of the report of receipt and expenditures?
