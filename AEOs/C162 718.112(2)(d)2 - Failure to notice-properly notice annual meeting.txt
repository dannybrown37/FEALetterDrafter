C162 718.112(2)(d)2 Notice Requirements- annual meeting



Allegation:  The board failed to notice or properly notice the annual meeting, in violation of section 718.112(2)(d) 2, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(d) 2, Florida Statutes

Elements:

When was the annual meeting held?
Was the notice mailed/hand-delivered and posted on the property?
When was the notice mailed/hand-delivered and posted?
Did the notice include an agenda?
Does the association have the affidavit verifying that the meeting notice was properly posted?
