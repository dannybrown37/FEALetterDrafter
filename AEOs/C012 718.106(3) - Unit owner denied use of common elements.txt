C012 718.106(3), Exclusive Possession

Allegation:  Unit owner was denied the exclusive right to use the common elements as provided by the declaration of condominium, in violation of section 718.106(3), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.106(3), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Did the unit owner attempt to use the common elements?

Was the unit owner denied use of the common elements?

Does the Declaration of Condominium provide for use of the common elements?
