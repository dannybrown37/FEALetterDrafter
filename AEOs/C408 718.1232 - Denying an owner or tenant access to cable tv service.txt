C408 718.1232 Denying an owner or tenant access to cable TV service

Allegation:  The Respondent denied an owner or tenant access to available cable television service, in violation of section 718.1232, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.1232, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Is cable television service available in the area where the condominium is located?

Did the owner and/or tenant try to obtain cable television service?

Did the board deny the owner and/or tenant access to the service?
