C216			718.112(2)(f), F.S.		 Annual budget 





Allegation:  Failed to budget common expenses in detail by accounts and expense classifications, including if applicable and not limited to those listed in s. 718.504(21),    in violation of section 718.112(2)(f), Florida Statute.

Association failed to adopt a separate budget of common expenses for each multi condominium and separate budget for association.

Failed to have schedule of limited common elements' costs in budget or attached for expenses applicable only to those entitle to use the limited common elements as provided for in s. 718.113(1).

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.112(2)(f), Florida Statute

Elements:  

Annual common expenses
Declaration of Condominium 
Financial Statements
