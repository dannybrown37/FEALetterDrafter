C159 718.112(2)(d)1 Failure to hold annual meeting



Allegation:  Failure to hold the annual meeting, in violation of section 718.112(2)(d) 1., Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(d) 1., Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was a notice of the annual meeting provided to unit owners?

What date was listed on the notice?

Are there minutes for the annual meeting?
