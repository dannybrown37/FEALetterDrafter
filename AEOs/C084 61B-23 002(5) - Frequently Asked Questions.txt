C084 61B-23.002(5) Frequently asked questions

Allegation:  The association failed to annually update the Frequently Asked Questions and Answers Sheet and/or the sheet does not substantially conform to BPR form 33-032, in violation of rule 61B-23.002(7) (a), Florida Administrative Code.

Statute/Rule Reference:  61B-23.002(7) (a), Florida Administrative Code

Elements:

Does the association have a completed Frequently Asked Questions and Answers Sheet?

When was the sheet last updated?

Does the sheet conform to BPR form 33-032?

Evidence Needed:

A copy of the association's most recent Frequently Asked Questions and Answers Sheet
