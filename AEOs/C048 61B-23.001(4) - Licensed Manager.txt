C048-61B-23.001(4)-Licensed Manager

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to employ a Licensed Manager, in violation of section 61B-23.001(4) FAC.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 61B-23.001(4) FAC.

Elements:
In furtherance of its fiduciary duty to the unit owners, a board of administration shall employ only a licensed community association manager where licensure is required by Section 468.431, Florida Statutes.
