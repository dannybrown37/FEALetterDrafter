C480 718.403(3) Developer Notification

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to notify existing unit owners of the decision not to add one or more additional phases, in violation of section 718.403(3), Florida Statutes.

Nature of Charge:

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.403(3), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Does the declaration or amendment (document) provide for the development of the condominium in phases?

Did the developer decided not to add one or more phases?

Did the developer notice existing unit owners of the decision?
