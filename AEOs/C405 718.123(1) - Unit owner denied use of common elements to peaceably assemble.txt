C405 718.123(1) Unit owner denied right to peaceable assemble in common areas

Allegation:  Unit owner was denied the exclusive right to peaceably assemble in the common elements as provided by the declaration of condominium, in violation of section 718.123(1), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.123(1), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Did the unit owner attempt to use the common elements to peaceably assemble?

Was the unit owner denied use of the common elements to peaceably assemble?
