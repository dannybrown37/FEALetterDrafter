C456 718.301(3) Developer assessment 

Allegation:  The association assessed the developer as a unit owner for capital improvements without the developer's written approval, in violation of section 718.301(3), Florida Statutes

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.301(3), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Does the developer still hold units that are for sale?

Did the association assess the developer for capital improvements?

Did the developer agree to the assessment in writing?

Evidence Needed:

Documentation showing that the developer still holds units for sale

A copy of the assessment notice for the capital improvements sent to the developer

A copy of the agreement, signed by the developer, agreeing to the assessment
