C147 718.112(2)(c) Failure to notice Board Meetings



Allegation:  Failure to notice a board meeting, in violation of section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was a board meeting held?

If yes, when was the meeting held?

Was a notice posted in the designated location on the condominium property?
