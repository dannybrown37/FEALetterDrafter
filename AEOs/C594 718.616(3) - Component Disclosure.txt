C594 718.616(3) Component disclosure

Allegation:  The developer of a converted property failed to provide the required disclosure of condition for the required existing components, in violation of section 718.616(3), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.616(3), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was the condominium created by converting existing property?

Did the developer disclose the condition for each of the required components that the existing improvements may include?

Were the disclosures made in writing?

Evidence Needed:

A copy of the disclosure(s) provided
