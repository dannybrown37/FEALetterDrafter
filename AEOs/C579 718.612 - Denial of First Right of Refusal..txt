C579 718.612 Denial of first right of Refusal

Allegation:  The Respondent denied a tenant the first right of refusal to purchase a unit being converted to condominium, in violation of section 718.612, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.612, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Is the property converting to condominium?

Was the complainant a tenant 180 days preceding a Notice of Intended Conversion?


Was the tenant denied the right to purchase a unit?
