C201 718.112(2)(d)4 Written agreements



Allegation:  The board failed to hold a unit owner meeting to obtain unit owner's approval when written agreements aren't authorized, in violation of section 718.112(2)(d)4, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(d) 4, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Do condominium documents allow for written agreements?
Was a unit owner meeting called?
Was it noticed?
