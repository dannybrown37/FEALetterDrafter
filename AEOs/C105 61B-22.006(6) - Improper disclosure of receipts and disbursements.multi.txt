C105 61B-22.006(6)(e), FAC. Reporting

Allegation 1: The association made improper disclosure of receipts and expenditures in the annual financial report in a multi-condominium association in violation of Rule 61B-22.006(6)(e), Florida Administrative Code.

Statute/Rule Reference:  61B-22.006(6)(e), FAC.

Elements:
* Are receipts and disbursements stated separately in the notes? 
* Is the way in which receipts and disbursements disclosed made clear on items not directly associated with any particular condominium?

Evidence:
Financial Statements
Declaration of Condominium
