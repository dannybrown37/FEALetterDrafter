C318 718.115(1)(a) Use of association funds for other than common expenses.

Allegation:  The association used budget moneys to pay for common expenses/services other than those statutorily defined or defined in the condominium documents, in violation of section 718.115(1)(a), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.115(1)(a), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Were budget moneys spent?

Were the moneys earmarked for common expenses/services?

Were the moneys spent on one of the common expenses/services outlined in the statute/documents?

Were the expenses/services provided on or after the date of turnover?
