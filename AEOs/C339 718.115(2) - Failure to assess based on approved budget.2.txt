C339 718.115(2)  Failure to assess based upon the approved budget

Allegation: Specifically, the complainant maintains that the amounts billed as monthly maintenance for 2005 are inconsistent with the approved 2005 operating budget.

Statute/Rule Reference: Section 718.115(2), Florida Statutes

Elements:  
* Were funds collected by assessments against the unit owners for payment of common expenses?
* Was the amount collected equal to the per unit amount of the approved budget?

Evidence Needed:
Approved Budget
Budget Meeting Minutes
Assessment Ledger
