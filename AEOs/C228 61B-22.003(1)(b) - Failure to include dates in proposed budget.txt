C228 61B-22.003(1)(b) Failure to disclose the beginning and ending dates in proposed budget.

Allegation:  Association failed to disclose the beginning and ending dates of the period covered in the proposed budget

Statute/Rule Reference:  Rule 61B-22.003(1)(b), FAC

Elements:
* Is date included?
* Is date correct pursuant to condominium documents?

Evidence Needed:
Proposed Budget
Declaration, Bylaws or Articles
