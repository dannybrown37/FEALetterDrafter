C381 61B-22.004(3) Improperly assessing unit owners.

Allegation:  Assessments were not properly assessed pursuant to the guarantee.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.116(9)(a), FS, 61B-22.004(3), FAC

Elements:
* What was is maximum level of assessments per unit type, as identified in the declaration?
* What was the total obligation of the unit owner as based on the adopted budget?
* What is the unit owner's proportionate ownership share of the common elements.
* Were the reserves waived?

Evidence Needed:
Declaration
Adopted budget
Meeting notes showing adoption of budget and/or waiving the reserves.
Document that the guarantee is stated. (Prospectus, Declaration or Purchase agreement)
