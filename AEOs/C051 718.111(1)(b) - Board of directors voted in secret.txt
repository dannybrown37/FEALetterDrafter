C051 718.111(1)(b) Improper vote by Directors at a board meeting

Allegation:  The Respondent conducted a vote of the board of directors in violation of Section 718.111(1)(b), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(1)(b), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Was a vote taken at a board meeting?

Was the vote by secret ballot?
