C474 718.403(1) Phasing Plan 

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to describe in the declaration of condominium in detail all anticipated phases in a condominium development, in violation of section 718.403(1), Florida Statutes.

NOTE:  Amendment to declaration of condominium can be inserted for declaration of condominium.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.403(1), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Does the original declaration of condominium submitting the initial phase to condominium ownership describe inn detail all anticipated phases?

Does the original declaration describe in detail the impact and the time period for competition for all anticipated phases?
