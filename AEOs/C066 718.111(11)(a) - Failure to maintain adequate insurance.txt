C066 718.111(11)(a) Obtain insurance

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to use its best efforts to obtain and maintain adequate insurance to protect the association, the association property, the common elements, and the condominium property in violation of section 718.111(11)(a), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(11)(a), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

*Does the association have proof of continuous insurance coverage?

*Does the association have an insurance policy that is currently in force? 

 *Is the insurance adequate?  

 *Did the association use its best efforts to obtain adequate insurance?
