C483 718.403(6) Additional Lands

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to include in the amendment to the declaration a legal description of the land being added to the condominium, in violation of section 718.403(6)(b), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.403(6)(b), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Was land added to the condominium?

Does the original declaration of condominium grant the right to add additional land to the condominium?

Was there an amendment adding additional land to the condominium?

Does the amendment include a legal description of the land being added to the condominium?
