C492 718.404(1) Commercial owner vetoed an amendment to the condo docs 

Allegation:  The Respondent allowed a commercial unit owner to veto an amendment to the condominium documents, in violation of section 718.404(1), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.404(1), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was an amendment to the condominium documents proposed and voted upon?

Was a commercial unit owner allowed to veto the amendment?
