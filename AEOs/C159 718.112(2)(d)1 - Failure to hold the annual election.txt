C159 718.112(2)(d)1 Failure to hold an annual meeting



Allegation:  Failure to hold the annual election, in violation of section 718.112(2)(d), Florida Statutes and rule 61B-23.0021(2), 

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(d), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was a notice sent to the owners for the annual election?

If yes, when was the election scheduled to be held?

How many vacancies were there on the board?

How many people submitted notices of intent?

Was the election meeting held?

Did 20% of the eligible voters submit ballots?

Were the ballots counted?
