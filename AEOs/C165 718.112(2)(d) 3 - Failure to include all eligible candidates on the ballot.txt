C165 718.112(2)(d)3 Failure to include all eligible candidates



Allegation:  Failure to include all eligible candidates on the ballot in violation of section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes and rule 61B-23.0021(9), Florida Administrative Code.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes

Elements:

When were candidates required to submit their notice of intent?

How many individuals submitted their notice by that date?

Were all of those people listed on the ballot?
