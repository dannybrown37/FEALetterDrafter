C165 718.112(2)(d)3 Including a candidate who did not provide a timely notice




Allegation:  Including a candidate who did not timely provide a notice of candidacy, in violation of section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes and rule 61B-23.0021(5), Florida Administrative Code.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was the annual election held?

When was the first notice of election sent out?

What date was given for candidates to submit their notice of intent?

Did any candidate submit a notice of intent after that date?

Was that person's name listed on the ballot?
