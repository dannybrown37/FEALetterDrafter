C300 718.113(1) Failure of the declaration to describe the method for apportioning costs of maintaining limited common elements.

Allegation:  Respondent failed to detail in the declaration the method for apportioning the costs of maintaining limited common elements, in violation of section 718.113(1) Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.113(1), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Is the area listed as limited common element? 

Do the documents state who is responsible for the maintenance of the limited common element? 

Has the association attempted to bill someone for the cost of maintaining the limited common element?

If yes, who?
