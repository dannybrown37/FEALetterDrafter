C147 718.112(2)(c) Non-emergency action taken at meeting



Allegation:  Non-emergency action taken at meeting (item not on agenda), in violation of section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was the meeting notice posted?

Did the notice include an agenda?

Do the meeting minutes reflect that action was taken on an item not on the agenda?
