C333 718.115(1)(e) Hurricane Shutters

Allegation:  The association failed to collect as a common expense the expense of installation, replacement operation, repair, and maintenance of hurricane shutters, in violation of section 718.115(1)(e), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.115(1)(e), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Did the association install, repair, operate, repair or maintain the project's hurricane shutters?

Was the expense of installation, replacement operation, repair, and maintenance of hurricane shutters assessed in proper percentages of ownership shares?
