C336 718.115(1)(f) Bad Debt

Allegation:  The association failed to collect unpaid shares of extinguished common expenses or extinguished assessments as a common expense of all unit owners in the condominium, in violation of section 718.115(1)(f), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.115(1)(f), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Were there unpaid common expenses or assessments extinguished by foreclosure of a superior lien or by deed in lieu of foreclosure?

Were all unit owners in the condominium assessed as a common expense the amount of the unpaid common expenses or assessments?
