C234 61B-22.003(5) Failure to show limited common elements expenses in proposed budget.

Allegation:  Association has not included applicable line items in the proposed budget

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(f)1., FS and rule 61B-22.003(5), FAC

Elements:
* Is item in question maintained by the association at the expense of only those unit owners entitled to use the limited common elements pursuant to section 718.113(1)?
* Is the item provided for in the condominium declaration?
* Is there a separate schedule, or schedules, for the expenses specific to each of the limited common elements?
* Are reserves required and included for each limited common element?

Evidence Needed:
Proposed Budget
Declaration
