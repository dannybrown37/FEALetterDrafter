C312 718.113(4) Failure to permit a unit owner to display a US flag

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to allow a unit owner to display a United States flag, in violation of section 718.113(4), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.113(4), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was the unit owner denied the right to display a United States flag?

Was the attempted display in compliance with allowable condominium rules governing the display of a United States flag?
