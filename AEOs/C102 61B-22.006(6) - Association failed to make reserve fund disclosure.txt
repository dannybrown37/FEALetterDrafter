C102 61B-22.006(3)(a)1.-4., FAC. Reporting

Allegation 1: The association failed to make significant reserve fund disclosures in the year-end financial statements or annual financial report in violation of Rule 61B-22.006(3)(a)1-4, Florida Administrative Code.

Statute/Rule Reference: 61B-22.006(3)(a)1.-4

Elements:
* Do the financial statements contain reserve fund disclosures?
* Do reserve disclosures contain the beginning balance in each reserve account as of the beginning of the fiscal period covered by the financial statements?
* Do reserve disclosures contain the amount of assessments and other additions to each reserve account including authorized transfers from other reserve accounts?
* Do reserve disclosures contain the amount expended or removed from each reserve account, including authorized transfers to other reserve accounts?
* Do reserve disclosures contain the ending balance in each reserve account as of the end of the fiscal period covered by the financial statements? 
* Is the manner in which reserve items where estimated, the date the estimates where last made, the associations policies for allocation reserve fund interest, and whether the reserve funds have been waived during the period covered by the financial statements disclosed?

Evidence:
Financial Statements
