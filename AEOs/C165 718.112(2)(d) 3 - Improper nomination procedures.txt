C165 718.112(2)(d)3 Improper nomination procedures




Allegation:  Improper nomination procedures used in election, in violation of section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes and rule 61B-23.0021(3), Florida Administrative Code.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(d) 3., Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was the annual election held?

Was a nomination committee appointed by the board?

Did the committee nominate anyone for election to the board?

Was that person placed on the board?
