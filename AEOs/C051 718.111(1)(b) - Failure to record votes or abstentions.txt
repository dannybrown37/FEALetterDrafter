C051 Failure to record votes or abstentions of a board member at a board meeting

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to record the votes or abstentions of board members at a board meeting in violation of section 718.111(1)(b), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference: Section 718.111(1)(b)., Florida Statutes.


Elements:

Was there a vote taken at a board meeting?

Were the votes and abstentions recorded?
