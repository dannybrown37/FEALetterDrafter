C228 61B-22.003(1)(e), (f) Failure to provide funding of one or more reserve categories in proposed budget.

Allegation:  Association failed to provide funding of one or more reserve categories in the proposed budget.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Rule 61B-22.003(1)(e), (f), and 61B-22.005(1)FAC

Elements:
* Does the reserve schedule include the required disclosures?
* Are all categories set forth as required by section 718.112(2)(f), FS. (or indicated as not applicable)
* Is the pooled funding method used?
* Was the proposed budget sent out to all unit owners before the reserves were waived or reduced?

Evidence Needed:
Proposed Budget
Meeting notice
