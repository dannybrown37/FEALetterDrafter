C342 718.115(4)(a) Multi-condominium assessment share

Allegation:  The board failed to assess multi condominium unit owners based upon proportionate share or as stated in the declaration of condominium, in violation of section 718.115(4)(a), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.115(4)(a), Florida Statutes

Elements: 

Is the condominium in the complaint a multi condominium?

Has the association proposed and/or adopted a budget?

How did the association determine how assessments were to be divided?

Evidence Needed:

A copy of the condominium declaration

A copy of the proposed and adopted budget
