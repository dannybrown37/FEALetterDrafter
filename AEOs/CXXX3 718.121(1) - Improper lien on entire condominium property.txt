CXXX 718.121(1) Failed to obtain unanimous consent prior to execution of a lien

Allegation:  TheRespondent, failed to obtain unanimous consent of the unit owners prior to allowing execution of a lien against the condominium as a whole, in violation of section 718.121(1), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.121(1), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Has the association entered into an agreement resulting in the execution of a lien?

Is the lien against the condominium as a whole rather than against individual condominium parcels?

Was there unanimous unit owner consent to the lien?
