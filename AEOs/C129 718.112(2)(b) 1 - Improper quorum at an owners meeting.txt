C129 Section 718.112(2)(b) 1 Quorum



Allegation:  The association failed to have a quorum at a unit owners' meeting, in violation of section 718.112(2)(b) 1, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(b) 1, Florida Statutes

Elements:

When was the unit owner meeting held?
Was it noticed?
How many units are in the condominium?
How many owners were present at the meeting either in person or by proxy?
