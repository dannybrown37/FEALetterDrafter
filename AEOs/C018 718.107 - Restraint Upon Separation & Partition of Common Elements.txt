C018 718.107 Restraint upon separation & partition of common elements

Allegation:  The Respondent conveyed a share in the common elements appurtenant to a unit separately from the unit, in violation of section 718.107(2), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.107, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was the common element transferred or encumbered?

Was the unit transferred or encumbered along with the common element?
