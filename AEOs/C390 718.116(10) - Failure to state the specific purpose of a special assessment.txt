C390 718.116(10) Failure to state the specific purpose of a special assessment

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to provide the specific purpose or purposes of a special assessment, in violation of section 718.116(10), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.116(10), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Did the association approve a special assessment?

Did the association send unit owners a notice regarding the special assessment?

Did the notice state the purpose of the special assessment?
