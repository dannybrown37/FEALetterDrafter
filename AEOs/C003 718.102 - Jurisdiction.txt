C003 718.102   Jurisdiction

Allegation:  Various behavior outside the division's jurisdiction.

Elements:

Is the allegation one, which if proven true, would be a violation of chapter 718, Florida Statutes or chapters 61B-15 through 25, 45 or 50?  

Does an AEO (Allegation Element Outline) exist for the issue?  

Is there an analogous issue for the alleged violation?  

Is there supporting documentation?  

Is the Respondent a regulated party under chapter 718, Florida Statutes?  

Evidence: N/A
