C225 718.112(2)(f)1. Failure to proposed budget.

Allegation:  Association has not included applicable line items in the proposed budget

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(f)1., FS

Elements:
* Are items listed in section 718.504(21), FS, included as applicable?
* Is item in question an association expense?
* Is the item provided for in the condominium documents?
