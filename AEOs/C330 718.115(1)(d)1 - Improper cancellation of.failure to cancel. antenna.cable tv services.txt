C330 718.115(1)(d) Cable TV

Allegation:  The association cancelled a contract for a master antenna television system or duly franchised cable television service without a proper vote, in violation of section 718.115(1)(d)1., Florida Statutes.

OR

The association failed to cancel a contract for a master antenna television system or duly franchised cable television service after a proper vote, in violation of section 718.115(1)(d)1., Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.115(1)(d)1., Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was the television service contract canceled?

Was there a vote taken to cancel the television service contract at the next regular or special meeting after the effective date of the contract?  

Was the vote to cancel carried by a majority of the voting interests present at the meeting?
