C096 Reporting 61B-22.006(1), FAC.

Allegation 1: The association failed to prepare year-end financial statements in accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) and/or failed to have reviewed or audited year-end financial statements prepared by a Florida licensed CPA in violation of Rule 61B-22.006(1), Florida Administrative Code.  

Statute/Rule Reference: 61B-22.006(1), FAC.

Elements:
* Have the financial statements been prepared?
* Are financial statements prepared by a CPA?
* Is there an auditors report signed by a licensed CPA?
* Are financial statements prepared according to Generally Accepted Accounting Procedures?
* Is there a statement in the Auditors report stating that GAAP was used?

Evidence:
Financial statements
