C147 718.112(2)(c) Failure to notice a committee meeting



Allegation:  Failure to properly notice and conduct a board/committee meeting, in violation of section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes.

Elements:


Was a board/committee meeting held?

If yes, when?

Was a notice posted in the designated location on the condominium property?

Did the notice include the required agenda?

Was a quorum of the board present for the meeting?
