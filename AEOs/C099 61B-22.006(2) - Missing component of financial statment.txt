C099 61B-22.006(2), FAC. Reporting

Allegation 1: The association failed to include one or more components of the year-end financial statements in violation of Rule 61B-22.006(2), Florida Administrative Code.  

Statute/Rule Reference: 61B-22.006(2), FAC.

Elements:
* Have the financial statements been prepared?
* Do the financial statements have an Accountant's or Auditor's Report?
* Do the financial statements have a balance sheet?
* Do the financial statements have a statement of revenues and expenses?
* Do the financial statements have a statement of changes in fund balances?
* Do the financial statements have a statement of cash flows?
* Do the financial statements have notes to financial statements?

Evidence:
Financial Statements
