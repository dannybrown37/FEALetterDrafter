C339 718.115(2), FS. Assessing

Allegation 1: The association failed to assess based upon proportionate to share or as stated in the declaration of condominium in violation of 718.115(2), FS.

Statute/Rule Reference: Section 718.115(2),

Elements:
* What is the amount being assessed?
* What is the division in the declaration of condominium?
* What percent of the assessment is being assessed against the owner?
* Does the percent match the declaration of Condominium?
* Are common expenses and common surplus the same as the unit owners Interest?

Evidence:
Declaration of Condominium
Billing statement of Owner
Budget with assessments to be billed to owners
