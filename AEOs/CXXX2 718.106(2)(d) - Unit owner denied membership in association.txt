C009 718.106(2)(d) Owner denied membership in association

Allegation:  Unit owner was denied membership in the condominium association, in violation of section 718.106(2)(d), Florida Statutes.


Nature of Charge:

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.106(2)(d), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Did the unit owner attempt to exercise any of his/her condominium association membership rights ?

Was the unit owner denied his/her right to exercise any of his/her condominium association membership rights?
