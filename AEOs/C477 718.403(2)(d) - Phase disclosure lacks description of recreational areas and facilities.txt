C477 718.403(2) Phase Declaration 

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to properly describe in the declaration of condominium the recreational areas and facilities which will be owned as common elements by all unit owners as each phase is added, in violation of section 718.403(2)(d), Florida Statutes.

NOTE:  Amendment to declaration of condominium can be inserted for declaration of condominium.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.403(2)(d), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Is this a phase condominium?

Does the declaration of condominium describe the recreational areas and facilities which will be owned as common elements as each phase is added?
