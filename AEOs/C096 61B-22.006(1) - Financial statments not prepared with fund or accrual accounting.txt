C096 61B-22.006(1), FAC. Reporting

Allegation 1: The association failed to prepare year-end financial statements using fund accounting and/or Failed to prepare year-end financial statements on accrual basis in violation of Rule 61B-22.006(1), Florida Administrative Code.  

Statute/Rule Reference: 61B-22.006(1), FAC.

Elements:
* Have the financial statements been prepared?
* Are financial statements prepared with fund accounting?
* Is there separation of operating expenses and revenues from reserve expenses and revenues?
* Are financial statements prepared on an accrual basis?
* Are expenses and revenues matched with period earned?

Evidence:
Financial statements
Budget
