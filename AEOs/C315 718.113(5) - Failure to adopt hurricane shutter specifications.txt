C315 718.113(5) Failure to adopt hurricane shutter specifications

Allegation:  The board failed to adopt hurricane shutter specifications, in violation of section 718.113(5), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.113(5), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Does the association have hurricane shutter specifications for each building operated by the association?

Do the specifications include color and style?

Did the complainant submit a request to the board to put up shutters?

Did the specification meet building codes?
