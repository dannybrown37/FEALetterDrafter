C228 61B-22.003(1)(c) Failure to disclose periodic assessments for each unit in proposed budget

Allegation: The complainant alleges that the 205 proposed budget fails to show the per unit assessment. 
Failure to disclose periodic assessments for each unit in proposed budget. 

Statute/Rule Reference:  61B-22.003(1)(c), Florida Administrative Code

Elements:  
* Was a budget proposed?
* Does the budget show the total assessment for each unit type according to proportion of ownership on a monthly basis or for any other period for which assessments will be due? 

Evidence Needed:
Proposed budget
