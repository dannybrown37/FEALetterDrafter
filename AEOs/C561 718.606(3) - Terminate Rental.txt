C561-718.606(3)-Terminate Rental

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to file the tenant to terminate their rental agreement, in violation of section 718.606(3), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.606(3), Florida Statutes.

Elements:
After the date of a notice of intended conversion, a tenant may terminate any rental agreement, or any extension period having an unexpired term of 180 days or less, upon 30 days' written notice to the developer. However, unless the rental agreement was entered into, extended, or renewed after the effective date of this part, the tenant may not unilaterally terminate the rental agreement but may unilaterally terminate any extension period having an unexpired term of 180 days or less upon 30 days' written notice.
