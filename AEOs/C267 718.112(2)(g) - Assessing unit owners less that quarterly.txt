C267 718.112(2)(g) Assessment/frequency

Allegation:  The board is assessing unit owners less frequently than quarterly, in violation of section 718.112(2)(g), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(g), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Has the association proposed/adopted a budget for current year?
Does the budget reflect the amount of assessments for each unit type?
What is the time period for assessments to be paid in the condominium documents?
