Case No.

C090 718.111(13)(a) Failure to provide year end audited financial statements

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to produce a complete set of year end audited financial statements, in violation of section 718.111(13)(a)3., Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(13)(a)3., Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Are there 50 or more units in the condominium?

Are the Association's total annual revenues $400,000 or more?

Were audited financial statements prepared?

Did the unit owners vote to prepare a report of cash receipts and expenditures or a reviewed or a compiled financial statement in lieu of the audited financial statements?
