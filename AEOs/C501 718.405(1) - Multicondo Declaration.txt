C501 718.405(1) Multicondo declaration

Allegation:  The declaration for each condominium operated by an association failed to include the required disclosures for participation in the multicondomium, in violation of section 718.405(1), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.405(1), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Does the association operate more than one condominium?

Is the project considered a multicondominium?

Was the multicondominium created on or after July 1, 2000?

Does the declaration contain the required disclosures?


Evidence Needed:

A copy of the declaration for each condominium operated by the association
