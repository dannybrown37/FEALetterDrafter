C396 718.119(1) Excess liability

Allegation:  The board exposed unit owners to excess liability, in violation of section 718.119(1), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.119(1), Florida Statutes

Elements:

What type of liability did the board expose the unit owners to?

How much liability where the unit owners exposed to?

How was the amount of liability divided among the owners?

Evidence Needed:

A copy of the condominium declaration and bylaws

Documentation showing the amount of liability the owners were exposed to and how it was divided
