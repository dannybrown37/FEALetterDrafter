C015 718.106(4) Tenants rights

Allegation:  Tennant of unit owner was denied the exclusive right to use the common elements as provided by the declaration of condominium, in violation of section 718.106(4), Florida Statutes.

NOTE:  Plan can be amended to included limited common elements by inserting the word limited before every reference to common elements.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.106(4), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Did the tenant of a unit owner attempt to use the common elements?

Was the tenant of the unit owner denied use of the common elements?
