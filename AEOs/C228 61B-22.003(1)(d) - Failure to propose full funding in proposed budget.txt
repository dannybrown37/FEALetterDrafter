C228 61B-22.003(1)(d) Failure to propose full funding in proposed budget.

Allegation:  Association failed to propose full funding in the proposed budget

Statute/Rule Reference:  Rule 61B-22.003(1)(d), FAC

Elements:
* Does the budget include a reserve schedule?
* Are all categories set forth as required by section 718.112(2)(f), FS. (or indicated as not applicable)
* Was the proposed budget sent out to all unit owners before the reserves were waived or reduced?

Evidence Needed:
Proposed Budget
Meeting notice
