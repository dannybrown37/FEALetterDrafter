C090 718.111(13) Failure to provide the annual financial report

Allegation:  The association failed to produce reviewed year end financial statements, in violation of section 718.111(13)(a)2., Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(13)(a)2., Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Does the Association have total annual revenues of less than $100,000?

Were reviewed year end financial statements prepared?

Did the unit owners vote to prepare a report of cash receipts and expenditures or a compiled financial statement in lieu of the reviewed financial statements?
