C222 61B-22.003(3) Record keeping

Allegation:  The minutes of the association failed to reflect the adoption of the budget and/or the association failed to maintain a copy of the proposed and adopted budget.

Statute/Rule Reference:  61B-22.003(3), Florida Administrative Code

Elements:

Did the association hold a meeting to adopt a budget for the fiscal year?

Do the minutes from the meeting reflect the adoption of the budget?

Does the association have a copy of the proposed and adopted budget in its official records?

Evidence Needed:

A copy of the meeting notice from the meeting where the budget was adopted

A copy of the meeting minutes from the meeting

A copy of the proposed and adopted budget
