C258 61B-22.005(6), F.A.C. - Timely funding of reserves.

Allegation:  Failure to timely fund reserves in the same frequency as assessments are due in violation of rule 61B-22.005(6), Florida Administrative Code.  

Statute/Rule Reference:  Rule 61B-22.005(6), F.A.C.

Elements:
* When are assessments due? Monthly or quarterly?
* Did the adopted budget include reserves?
* And how much should have been funded per month/quarter?
* Were the reserves funded in the proper frequency 

Evidence Needed
Adopted budgets for the years in question? 
Bank statements for the years in question?
