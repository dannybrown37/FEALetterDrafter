C102 61B-22.006(3)(d)  Improper disclosure of revenue and expenses for limited common elements

Allegation:  Improper disclosure in the year-end financial statement of revenues and expenses relating to limited common elements.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Rule 61B-22.006(d), FS

Elements:
* Does the association maintain the limited common elements in question?
* What method is provided for allocation for revenues and expenses for limited common elements pursuant to the declaration or other condominium documents?
* Which method is use in the financial statements?
* Are there any amendments that have changed the method?
* Did the previous reports use the correct method?
* Are the expenses apportioned to those unit owners entitled to exclusive use?

Evidence Needed:
Declaration and any amendments or other docs
Current financial report
Previous financial report
Budget and limited common element reserve schedule
