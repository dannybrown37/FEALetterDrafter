C621 718.62 Discrimination against non-purchasing tenants

Allegation:  Respondent failed to grant existing tenants in a conversion condominium the same rights, privileges, and services enjoyed before the date of the written Notice of Conversion and that are granted, offered or provided to purchasers, in violation of section 718.62, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.62, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Is the property converting to condominium?

Was the complaining tenant provided a Notice of Intended Conversion?

Is the complaining tenant being denied rights, privileges, and services enjoyed before the date of the written Notice of Conversion?

Are purchasers of units being granted, offered, or provided these rights, privileges, and services?
