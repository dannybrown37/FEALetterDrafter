CXXX 718.403(4)  
Allegation:  Unit owners were not given 100 percent ownership of the common elements within developed phases when one or more phase were not built, in violation of section 718.403(4), Florida Statutes.


Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.403(4), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Does the declaration or amendment (document) provide for the development of the condominium in phases?

Does the document describe each unit's percentage of ownership in the common elements?

Were one or more phases not developed?

Were unit owners given 100 percent ownership of the common elements within developed phases added as part of the condominium?
