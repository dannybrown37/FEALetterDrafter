C573-718.608(2)-Contents NOIC

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to provide an NOIC with all required disclosures, in violation of section 718.608(2), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.608(2), Florida Statutes.

Elements:
IF YOUR RENTAL AGREEMENT EXPIRES IN THE NEXT 45 DAYS, you may extend your rental agreement for up to 45 days after the date of this notice while you decide whether to extend your rental agreement as explained above. To do so, you must notify the developer in writing. You will then have the full 45 days to decide whether to extend your rental agreement as explained above.
