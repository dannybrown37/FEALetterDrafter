C132 Section 718.112(2)(b)2 Limited Proxies



Allegation:  Failure of proxy to contain required elements, in violation of section 718.112(2)(b) 2, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(b) 2, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was a proxy used?
Was it a general or limited proxy?
Does the limited proxy conform to the proxy form adopted by the Division?
