C102 61B-22.006(3)(a)5  Failure to disclose manner of reserve estimates and date in the annual financial report.

Allegation:  The financial report did not disclose the date that the estimates for the reserves were last made and did not disclose the manner by which the reserves items were estimated.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Rule 61B-22.006(3)(a)5.,, FS

Elements:
* Were reserves estimated?
* Did report include notes?
* Did notes mention how reserves were estimated? When?
* Was interest policies stated?
* Did the notes state if the reserves were to be waived?

Evidence Needed:
Current financial report
Previous financial report
