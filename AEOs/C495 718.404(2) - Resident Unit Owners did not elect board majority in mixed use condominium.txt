C495 718.404(2) Resident Unit Owners did not elect board majority in mixed use condominium

Allegation:  The Respondent did not allow the resident unit owners to vote for a majority of the seats on the board of administration in a mixed-use condominium where the residential units make up 50 percent or more of the units, in violation of section 718.404(2), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.404(2), Florida Statutes

Elements:

How many members are on the board?

How many of these board members were voted for by the resident unit owners?

Are 50 percent or more of the units in the condominium residential?
