C147 718.112(2)(c) Failure to Post agenda



Allegation:  Failure to post the agenda for a board meeting, in violation of section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was a board meeting held?

Was a notice posted for the meeting?

Did the notice include an agenda?
