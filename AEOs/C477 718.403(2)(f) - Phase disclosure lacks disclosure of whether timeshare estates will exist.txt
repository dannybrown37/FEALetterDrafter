C477 718.403(2) Phase Declaration 

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to properly disclose whether or not timeshare estates will or may be created with respect to units in any phase, in violation of section 718.403(2)(f), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.403(2)(f), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Does the declaration or amendment (document) provide for the development of the condominium in phases?

Does the document indicate whether or not timeshare estates will or may be created with respect to units in any phase?
