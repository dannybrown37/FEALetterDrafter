C 718.618(3)(b), FS. Converter Reserves


Allegation 1: The association is improperly using converter reserves in violation of Section 718.618(3)(b), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference: Section 718.618(3)(b), FS

Elements:
* What was the money spent on?
* What was the money supposed to be spent on?
* Has converter reserves been used for something other than intended purpose?
* Was there a vote by unit owners of 3/4 to use the money for other than its intended purpose?
* How will the money be replaced?

Evidence:
Budget
Financial Statements 
Bank statements
Declaration of Condominiums
Billing Statements
