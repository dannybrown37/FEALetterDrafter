C552 718.504(21) Budget

Allegation:  The prospectus/offering circular provided to buyers failed to include a budget or the budget provided failed to include the required line items, in violation of section 718.504(21), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  718.504(21), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Does the condominium include more than 20 units?

Was a prospectus or offering circular provided to buyers?

Did the prospectus or offering circular include a budget?

Did the budget include the required line items?

Evidence Needed:

A copy of the prospectus/offering circular

A copy of the budget included with the prospectus/offering circular?
