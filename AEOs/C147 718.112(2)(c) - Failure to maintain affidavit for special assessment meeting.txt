C147 Section 718.112(2)(c) Failure to maintain affidavit- special assessment meeting



Allegation:  The board failed maintain affidavit by person who gave notice of special assessment meeting, in violation of section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was a meeting held to consider a special assessment?

Was a notice provided to the unit owners?

Was an affidavit done to show when the notice was sent to the owners?

Does the association have the affidavit?
