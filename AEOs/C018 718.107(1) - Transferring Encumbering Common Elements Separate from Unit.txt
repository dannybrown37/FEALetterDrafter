C018 718.107(1) Transferring Encumbering common elements separate for unit

Allegation:  Respondent conveyed or encumbered a share in the common elements appurtenant to a unit separately from the unit, in violation of section 718.107(1), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.107(2), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was a share in the common elements transferred or encumbered?

Was the unit transferred or encumbered along with the share of the common elements?
