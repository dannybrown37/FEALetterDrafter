C441 61B-23.003(5) 10 day notice

Allegation:  The developer failed to notify the division within 10 days of the name and mailing address of the first unit owner elected to the board, in violation of rule 61B-23.003(5), Florida Administrative Code.

Statute/Rule Reference:  61B-23.003(5), Florida Administrative Code

Elements:

Has the first non-developer unit owner been elected to the board?

When was the unit owner elected to the board?

Did the developer notify the division of the person's name and mailing address?

Evidence Needed:

List of board members

Copy of the minutes from the meeting where the person was elected to the board

Copy of the letter (or other document) sent by the developer to the Division providing the person's name and address
