C147 section 718.112(2)(c) Failure to allow unit owners to speak
 


Allegation:  Failure to allow unit owners to speak at a board meeting, in violation of section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112(2)(c), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Was a board meeting held?

Was a notice posted for the meeting?

Were the owners allowed to speak on agenda items before the meeting was adjourned?

If yes, do the meeting minutes reflect this?
