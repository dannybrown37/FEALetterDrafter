C009 718.106(2)(b)  Exclusive right to Transfer use rights of  limited common elements

Allegation:  Unit owner was denied the right to transfer his/her exclusive right to use the common elements as provided by the declaration of condominium, in violation of section 718.106(2)(b), Florida Statutes.

Nature of Charge:

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.106(2)(b), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Did the unit owner attempt to transfer his/her right to use the common elements?

Was the unit owner denied his/her right to transfer use of the common elements?

Was the intended transferee denied the right to use the common elements because the transfer of rights was not recognized by the association?
