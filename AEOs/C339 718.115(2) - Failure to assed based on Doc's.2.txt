C339 718.115(2) Failed to assess unit owners in their proportionate share of ownership

Allegation: It is alleged that the Association failed to assess unit owners in their proportionate share of ownership.

Statute/Rule Reference: Section 718.115(2), Florida Statutes.  

Elements:  
* Research county records for declaration, amendments or consolidation efforts by the association to determine if this is a multi condo or standard condo.
* Determine the method that should be used pursuant to the Declaration.
* Determine how the calculations were done.
* Provide direction to association on how to properly calculate assessments based on declaration of condominium.

Evidence Needed:
Copy of recorded declaration
Copy of recorded amendments
Copy of corrected 2005 budget?
