C360 718.116(9) Improperly excusing a unit owner from paying assessments

Allegation:  The board improperly excused a unit owner from paying his/her share of the common expenses, in violation of section 718.116(9), Florida Statutes.

NOTE:  May substitute special assessment for common expense.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.116(9), Florida Statutes

Elements:

Were unit owner shares of common expenses collected?

Did the board excuse one or more unit owners from paying the assessment?
