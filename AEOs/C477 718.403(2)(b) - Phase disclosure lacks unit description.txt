C477 718.403(2) Phase Declaration 

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to properly describe in the declaration of condominium the units to be included in each phase of the project, in violation of section 718.403(2)(b), Florida Statutes.

NOTE:  Amendment to declaration of condominium can be inserted for declaration of condominium.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.403(2)(b), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Is this a phase condominium?

Does the declaration of condominium disclose the minimum and maximum number of units to be included in each phase?

Does the declaration of condominium disclose the general size of units to be included in each phase?
