C081 718.111(12)(d) Failure to prepare Q and A sheet

Allegation:   The Respondent failed to prepare a Question & Answer sheet, in violation of section 718.111(12)(d), Florida Statutes.

OR

The Respondent failed to update the Question & Answer sheet annually, in violation of section 718.111(12)(d), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.111(12)(d), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Does the condominium contain more than 20 residential units?

Was a "Frequently asked Question and Answer" sheet prepared?
OR
Was the "Frequently asked Question and Answer" sheet updated on an annual basis?
