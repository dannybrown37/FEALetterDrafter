C483 Additional Lands

Allegation:  The Respondent failed to include a statement in the amendment to the declaration of condominium submitting acquired additional land to the condominium ownership as an addition to the condominium, in violation of section 718.403(6)(a), Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.403(6)(a), Florida Statutes.

Elements:

Was land added to the condominium?

Does the original declaration of condominium grant the right to add additional land to the condominium?

Was there an amendment adding additional land to the condominium?

Does the amendment include a statement submitting acquired additional land to the condominium ownership as an addition to the condominium?
