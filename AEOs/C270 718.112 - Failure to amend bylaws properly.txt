C270 Section 718.112(2)(h)2 Bylaws Admendment

Allegation:  The association failed to properly amend bylaws, in violation of section 718.112, Florida Statutes.

Statute/Rule Reference:  Section 718.112, Florida Statutes

Elements:

Were the bylaws amended?
Was the amendment sent to the unit owners?
Did the amendment contain the fill text?
Were additions and deletions underlined or struck through?
Did the unit owners vote to amend bylaws?
Was the required vote obtained?
Was amendment recorded with clerk's office?
